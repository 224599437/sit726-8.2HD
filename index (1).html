Phishing Detection

⏱ 01:45
From: UniAdmin urgent@verifyme.net To: You <student@deakin.edu.au>
SUBJECT

Action Required: Verify Your University Credentials Immediately

Dear Student,


Our records show your account will be SUSPENDED within 24 hours if you do not verify your credentials. Failure to act will result in permanent loss of access to all university services.


Please confirm now: http://deakin-verify.ru/login


📄 verification_form.html
Is this email safe or a threat?
🎉

Correct! That's a Phishing Email

Red flags: Sender domain verifyme.net (not deakin.edu.au), .ru login URL, urgency tactics, and an HTML attachment. Real universities never send credential verification requests this way.

+150 XP Earned!
❌

Not Quite — This Was a Threat

Key red flags: Sender domain verifyme.net, .ru URL, suspension urgency, and HTML attachment. Review the Phishing 101 module to sharpen your detection skills.
